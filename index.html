Quote Spark

Quote Spark is a beautifully designed Flutter application that brings daily inspiration to users. The app allows users to explore and read motivational, life, and love quotes through both offline list-based content and online API-powered updates. It ensures users can access quotes anytime — even without an internet connection.

The app features a clean and responsive UI, smooth navigation, and efficient local storage handling for offline mode. Online mode seamlessly fetches new quotes from APIs, keeping the content fresh and engaging. Perfect for those seeking motivation or positivity throughout their day.

Decido

Decido is a simple and efficient offline decision-making app built with Flutter. It lets users add multiple options and instantly generates a random choice, helping them decide quickly. All data is stored locally using SharedPreferences, ensuring privacy and a smooth, lightweight experience.
